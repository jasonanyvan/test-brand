Primary logo

The logo should, where possible, appear in ‘Picton Blue’ against a ‘White’ background. If and when written out it, the logo should be stylized as AnyVan

Applications

Primary logomark

In some instances, a reduced version of the logo is required. This should be seen as an fallback and should only be used...

  • if the AnyVan brand and context has been clearly established
  • In environments clearly controlled by AnyVan
  • When spatial restrictions make the full logo illegible

Clear space

When using logo assets with other graphic elements or text, make sure you give it enough room to breathe. The clear space around the logo assets should be 100% of the height of the assets, marked below as ‘j’ and 'r'

Minimum size

To ensure legibility of the assets, minimum sizes should be adhered to. As indicated below, the height of the logo assets should not be smaller than 25px - the only exception to this rule is if the logomark is used as a favicon

Incorrect usage

The primary logo and logomark should always appear flat and should never be embellished with shadows, bevels or embossing techniques

The primary logomark should never be used as anything other than a logomark and a primary branding mark

Social icons

Social media icons are individually designed based on each platform specifications. They are an exception to the clear space guidelines and are sized optically to best fit each shape. As a general rule, the logomark height should be 40% of the height of container

Remember to align the logo center vertically and horizontally of the icon shape

Favicons

At such a small size / scale, the AnyVan logomark can be simplified even futher. Removing the inner detailing of the glyph doesn't reduce the recognition of the mark, but it does aid in keeping the favicons cleaner and more legible.
