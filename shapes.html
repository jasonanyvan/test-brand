Truncated parallelogram

Using a range of between 17% - 34% of the height of the shape as a guide for the corner radius allows us to mimic the corner radius of the logmark

Rules

Shapes that stray too far from the logomark should also be avoided

Placement

Shapes should always sit flush with the edge of the artwork

Sharp edges should never be seen - there should always be the illusion that there is more to the shape beyond the boundaries of the artwork
